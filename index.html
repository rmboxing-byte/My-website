Step Into The Ring

A nonprofit organization dedicated to winning against depression and anxiety. Through boxing, mentorship, and coaching—we're building mental health support for veterans, low-income families, and our community.

Learn More

Our Mission

We provide a supportive environment for all male and female athletes of all ages who are interested in pursuing competitive or non-competitive boxing training. Our club's friendly atmosphere allows all members to foster discipline, increase athleticism, encourage healthy living, and build a strong sense of community.

We believe mental wellness is a journey best taken together. Through boxing, mentorship, and genuine human connection, we provide accessible mental health support to those who need it most—with one core principle guiding everything we do.

Our Programs

🥊

Boxing Classes

Build strength, confidence, and resilience through expert-led boxing instruction designed for all fitness levels. Release stress and anxiety with every punch in our group classes.

👥

One-on-One Training

Get personalized attention with individual training sessions tailored to your goals, fitness level, and mental health journey.

💪

Strength & Conditioning

Build functional strength and endurance with guided conditioning programs designed to improve both physical and mental resilience.

🍎

Meal Planning

Nutrition guidance and personalized meal plans to fuel your body and support your mental health recovery.

Our coaches provide mentorship, counseling referrals, and community support to help you manage stress, anxiety, and depression effectively.

Who We Serve

Veterans

We provide veterans with a supportive community, physical training, and mental health resources to help transition successfully into civilian life.
